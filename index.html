Rules & Regulations

🏆 GO PARK Sports × The One – Padel Summer League 2026

A summer padel league running from 11 July to 4 October 2026, played on Saturdays and Sundays between 7 PM and 11 PM. Teams compete in a group stage followed by two parallel knockout competitions.

Quick Reference
📅 Dates: 11 July – 4 October 2026
⏰ Saturdays & Sundays, 7–11 PM (4 × 1-hour slots per court per night)
💰 Entry fee: HKD 3,500 per team
👥 Team size: 2–5 players, aged 18–65
🎾 2 players on court per match; subs rotate between matches only
📦 3 new tubes of balls per team; 1 shirt per participant

Phase 1 — Group Stage

  • Teams are divided into groups. Every team plays every other team in their group once (round-robin).
  • Points system: Win = 3 pts, Draw = 1 pt, Loss = 0 pts.
  • Top 4 teams from each group advance to the Champions Knockout.
  • 5th place and below enter the Phoenix Cup.

Phase 2A — Champions Knockout

  • All Champions Knockout qualifiers play a full round-robin among themselves.
  • After the round-robin, the top 2 teams play a Championship Final.
  • The Final winner is the Champions Knockout Champion.
Full knockout rules will be communicated by the league organisers before the phase begins.

Phase 2B — Phoenix Cup

  • All Phoenix Cup qualifiers (5th place and below from each group) play a full round-robin among themselves.
  • After the round-robin, the top 2 teams play the Phoenix Cup Final.
Same format as the Champions Knockout, but separate competition. No relegation — this is a second chance to win something.

📊 Points System

  • Win = 3 points
  • Draw = 1 point
  • Loss = 0 points

⏱ Time Limit & Games Rule

Each match is 60 minutes total including warm-up. If the clock expires before two sets are completed, total games won decide the match result:

  • Example 1: Score is 6–3, 2–4 at time → Games: 8–7 → Team A wins (3 pts)
  • Example 2: Score is 6–3, 3–6 at time → Games: 9–9 → Draw (1 pt each)
If time expires mid-set, count only completed games. The incomplete set does not count.

📏 Tiebreaker Order (equal points)

Applied in strict order — move to the next criterion only if still tied:

  1. Head-to-head points (between the tied teams only)
  2. Head-to-head game difference (between the tied teams only)
  3. Overall game difference (all group matches)
  4. Total games won (all group matches)
  5. Coin toss — witnessed by coach
Numbers only. No subjective calls. The coach is the final arbiter and the same rules apply to everyone.

⏱ Match Clock

  • 60 minutes total, including warm-up. Clock starts at the scheduled time — not when you walk on.
  • Late arrival: you play with whatever time is remaining.
  • Less than 40 minutes remaining when you arrive? The match is rescheduled.
  • Rain interruption: if fewer than 40 minutes were played before rain, both captains must reschedule.

🎾 Match Format

  • Format: 2 full sets + super tie-break (if time allows).
  • Super tie-break: first to 10 points, win by 2.
  • If time expires before 2 sets are complete: total games decide (see Scoring above).
  • Players may also book courts privately at their own cost on weekdays to practice — this does not count as a league match.

⚠️ Why No Challenges

  • Every scenario is predefined. No subjective calls — only games and the clock.
  • The coach is the final arbiter.
  • The same rules apply equally to everyone, every match.

📅 Booking a Match

  • Both captains agree on a date and time: Saturdays or Sundays, 7–11 PM only.
  • Both captains confirm with the coaches — slot is then locked.
  • Court booking is first come, first served.
  • Missing Week 1 or 2 is fine — the league runs until 4 October.

🔁 Rescheduling

  • No unilateral changes. Rescheduling requires both captains + coach approval, at least 48 hours in advance.
  • Late arrival with less than 40 minutes remaining = automatic reschedule.
  • Rain with less than 40 minutes played = both captains must reschedule.
Failure to reschedule properly or to play your scheduled matches is a breach of the Captain's Code.

👥 Team Composition

  • Team size: 2 to 5 registered players.
  • Age range: 18–65 years.
  • 2 players on court per match at all times.
  • Substitutions are allowed between matches only — not during a match.
  • All players must be registered on the team roster.

🎽 Equipment

  • 3 new tubes of balls provided per team for the season.
  • 1 shirt per participant included with registration.
  • Entry fee: HKD 3,500 per team.

⚠️ Captain's Code — No Drama, No Complaints

  • By signing up, you and your team commit to playing ALL your scheduled matches. Responsibility is on you.
  • No complaints about the format, rules, or schedule. Every rule applies equally to everyone.
  • For the knockout stage: Captains must ensure all team players are available for the knockout day.
You don't have to like every rule — you only have to follow it.

🏅 Spirit of the League

This league is built on fair play, shared responsibility, and competitive fun. The coach's decision is final. Disputes are resolved by the numbers — games and the clock — not arguments. LET'S GO ONERS! TIME TO SMASH THIS SUMMER 🔥
